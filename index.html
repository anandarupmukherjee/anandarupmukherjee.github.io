I am currently pursuing PhD in Engineering, from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur). My current designation is Senior Research Fellow (SRF) and I am engaged  with the Department of Agriculture and Food Engineering (AgFE) and School of Information Technology (SIT) at  IIT Kharagpur.

I volunteer as a part of of the Membership development team at the IEEE Student branch at IIT Kharagpur.

I finished my M.Tech and B.Tech from West Bengal University of Technology in the years 2012 and 2010, respectively.

I have served as an Assistant professor in the department of Electronics & Communication Engineering at University of Engineering & Management, Jaipur (2012-2014); prior to that I served as a lecturer in the department of Information technology at Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata (2011-2012).
