Research Interest Profile

I work in the broad domain of networks, with special interest in Aerial Sensor Networks, Smartphone Networks, Low- power Wireless Sensor Networks, Smart Wearables and Internet of Things.

  • UAV Stabilization and Control
  • UAV Imaging
  • UAV Networks

Agricultural Systems

  • Agri-Intelligence for IoT systems
  • Smart Monitoring Systems

Battlefield Systems

Smartphone Systems
